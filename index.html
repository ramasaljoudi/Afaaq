AFAAQ

An interactive gamified journey to explore Expo 2030 Riyadh

Problem

Visitors often experience exhibitions in a passive and traditional way, which reduces engagement and limits full exploration of the event.

Vision

Transform the visitor journey into an immersive and engaging experience by combining culture, technology, and interactive gameplay.

Solution

A gamified application that motivates users to explore pavilions through challenges, rewards, and interactive experiences.

Saudi Spirit

The experience reflects the diversity of Saudi regions, offering a unique cultural journey inspired by heritage and identity.
